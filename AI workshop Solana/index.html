Resources & Best Practices

Best Practices for AI Tool Combinations

  • Use LLMs for initial content, then feed it into presentation tools like Gamma.
  • Generate visual assets with image AIs to use in Canva/Designer mockups.
  • Combine AI-analyzed data insights with AI video tools for compelling reports.
  • Always start with a clear prompt and iteratively refine it.
  • Remember that AI is an assistant. Human oversight and creativity are essential.

Key Discussion Points

  • Technical limitations and capabilities of current AI tools.
  • The importance of security audits for any AI-generated code, especially Solana programs.
  • Ethical considerations, including data privacy and potential biases in AI models.
  • Creative workflows that blend multiple AI tools to achieve complex results.
  • The future of AI in dApp development and its impact on developer roles.
