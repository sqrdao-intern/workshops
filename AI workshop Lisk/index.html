LLMs can act as powerful brainstorming partners, helping you explore diverse concepts, identify potential challenges, and fine-tune your thoughts into actionable plans. DeepSeek

Resources & Best Practices

Best Practices for AI Tool Combinations

  • Use AI text generators for initial content, then feed it into presentation tools like Gamma.
  • Generate visual assets (icons, backgrounds) with image AIs to use in Canva/Microsoft Designer/Imagen mockups.
  • Combine AI-analyzed data insights with AI video tools like Veo 3 for compelling data reports.
  • Always start with a clear prompt and iteratively refine it. Don't expect perfect results on the first try.
  • Remember that AI is an assistant. Human oversight, critical thinking, and creativity are essential.
